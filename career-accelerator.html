Who It’s For

People navigating change — between roles, re-entering the workforce, or aiming to move away from a role that no longer fits.

Program Structure

Week 1 — Resume

Tell your story with clarity and measurable results — plus AI-powered resume insights and optimization.

Give the Cohort as a Gift

You can also purchase enrollment as a gift for someone who needs career support: a friend in transition, a family member graduating, or a colleague you want to support.

How gifting works

  1. Go to the application Google Form and select “It's a gift for someone else.”
  2. Provide the recipient’s name and email address.
  3. You can optionally add a short gift message that we’ll include in their welcome email.
  4. If they’re a good fit for the cohort, we’ll send them next steps and a payment link (or coordinate with you, depending on what you select in the form).

This is a great way to give someone not just advice, but a structured path and community to move their career forward.

Common Questions

Who is this cohort for?

Mid-career professionals, emerging leaders, and people in transition who want a more intentional, strategic approach to their next role—not just firing off more applications.

Can I join if I’m already employed?

Yes. Many participants are currently employed but exploring what’s next, getting ready for internal moves, or wanting to be more intentional about their career.

What if I can’t attend one of the live sessions?

Sessions are recorded, and you can watch the replay. To qualify for the money-back guarantee, you’ll need to attend or watch all sessions and complete the assignments.

How do refunds work?

If you meet the guarantee conditions and request a refund within 7 days of the final session, I’ll refund 100% of your tuition to your original payment method.
