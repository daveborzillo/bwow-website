2-Day Live Course

Registered Scrum Master

A practical, hands-on Scrum course where you learn by doing — designed for people who want to lead teams using modern Agile practices, not just pass an exam.

View Dates & Register Get Updates on Future Classes

Who This Course Is For

  • New or aspiring Scrum Masters who want a strong foundation.
  • Team leads, project managers, or engineers transitioning into Agile roles.
  • Leaders who want to understand how Scrum really works in practice.

What You’ll Learn

  • Scrum roles, events, and artifacts — and how they support real delivery.
  • How to facilitate sprint planning, daily scrums, reviews, and retrospectives.
  • How to help teams self-organize, improve flow, and remove impediments.
  • How to work with product owners, stakeholders, and leadership.

Course Format & Logistics

  • Duration: 2 consecutive days (live, virtual).
  • Format: Short lessons, group discussion, and team exercises.
  • Focus: Real-world scenarios, not just theory or exam prep.
  • Extras: Templates and facilitation tools you can use right away.

After registering, you’ll receive calendar invites, pre-course instructions, and links to the collaboration tools we’ll use in class.

Learn From a Practicing Agile Coach

This course is taught by Dave Borzillo, an experienced Agile coach who has helped leadership teams and delivery teams adopt Scrum and better ways of working in complex environments.

You’ll hear real examples from the field, get honest answers to your questions, and leave with a clearer idea of how to use Scrum in your own context — not an idealized textbook version.

Ready to Lead With Confidence?

If you’re ready to move beyond buzzwords and learn how Scrum actually helps teams deliver, this course is for you.
