Meet the Founder

I'm Dave Borzillo, helping you move from frustration to momentum.

I help leaders, teams, and organizations discover practical, modern approaches to collaboration, communication, and getting meaningful work done.

For more than a decade, I’ve coached executives, guided complex transformations, and led teams recognized as internal disruptors inside Fortune 500 companies. My work blends Agile, Lean, facilitation, leadership development, and systems thinking into clear, actionable methods.

What I Do

  • Advise leaders and teams to collaborate more effectively
  • Teach frameworks like Liberating Structures and Scrum
  • Run cohort-based programs like the Better Career Cohort
  • Guide strategic alignment and forward progress
  • Write about modern ways of working, including my book Who Killed Agile?

My Approach

I focus on Practicality over theory—everything I teach works in real environments. I prioritize simplicity because clarity creates confidence, and I'm obsessed with Flow—reducing the delays and stalled decisions that burn people out.

Navigating the Future with AI

Artificial intelligence isn’t just a trend—it's a new layer of how work gets done. I’ve worked on knowledge-based systems since the late 90s, and the goal remains the same: capture expertise and make complex work easier.

Today, I help teams integrate AI into their day-to-day flow in practical ways that reduce friction and free people to focus on meaningful, human-centered work.
